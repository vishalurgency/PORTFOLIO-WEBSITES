A Cyber Security Engineer is a professional responsible for designing, implementing, and managing the security measures to protect computer systems, networks, and data from cyber threats. About Me

A Network Engineer is a skilled professional responsible for designing, building, and maintaining computer networks, including local area networks (LANs), wide area networks (WANs), and the Internet. They ensure that data communication networks are secure, efficient, and running smoothly, allowing organizations to communicate effectively and access resources. Network Engineers are also known as Network Architects, Network Administrators, or Network Technicians, and their primary goal is to provide a stable and high-performance network infrastructure that meets the needs of the organization.
